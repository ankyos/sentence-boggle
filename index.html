How to Play

One device = one local team session. It saves only on this iPad/browser.

Class roles

  • Pointer: finds useful words on the board.
  • Tapper: taps the words (can stay quiet).
  • Coach: picks the sentence that sounds natural.
  • Speaker: reads it in the finale.

How scoring works

  • Each kept sentence: +1
  • Uses because / but / so: +1
  • Is a question: +1
  • Peer vote (once): +3
  • Used words disappear, so points come from new sentences.

Parts of speech

Teacher note

No adjacency rule: students tap any words in any order, then press Build sentence and choose from a few fully natural sentences/questions. Used words grey out, so they spread across the board. Finale unlocks at 3+ sentences and 1 goal — not all goals. Use New tiles for a fresh board, New mission for a new topic. Resize the board by editing BOARD_COLS / BOARD_ROWS.
